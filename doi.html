Office of the Director of National Intelligence

Over the summer of 2023, I got the incredible opportunity to work under the DOI as a USC Intelligence Community Center for Academic Excellence Scholar. During my internship, I contributed to the preparation of a comprehensive report, ensuring the inclusion of data-driven insights and recommendations. The report addressed various aspects, including risks to space assets, cyber vulnerabilities, and the state of quantum computing.

My Role
I was involved in analysis and research of a large data set. Using those results, Our team was tasked with the presentation of findings to academic and industry stakeholders, emphasizing the strategic implications of quantum technologies in the context of national security. I developed skills in strategic planning and foresight, essential for developing effective strategies that anticipate trends and stakeholder needs.
My Impact
My contributions to the report led to strategic recommendations that can influence future policies and security measures for space assets. The presentation led to productive discussion to enhance decision making.
KEY LEARNINGS
Collaboration
Be proactive
Through this internship, I learned the importance of humility; understanding that no matter how much I learn, there is so much value in discussion and critiques from collegues and stakeholders. I also found it helpful to be curious in understanding other projects and how other teams operate.
One big learning was that there is no right answer when it comes to data; a lot of it falls under a gray area, and it comes down to making smart decisions based on different perspectives. I learned to rely on my human resources: my team, my manager, and my acumen to find the relevant results and overall be proactive in my approach.
KEY LEARNINGS
Communication and Insights
Soft data skills
While composing our report and presentation to showcase our findings my ability to articulate complex technical content in an accessible manner was honed. In marketing, communicating effectively with diverse audiences, including customers, stakeholders, and team members, is vital.
My role in this project has significantly sharpened my analytical skills and strategic thinking. I dealt with a lot of data and through analysis, I extracted key insights that were not only relevant for the project but also showcased my ability to distill information into understandable and actionable points. In a marketing role, these skills translate into the ability to analyze market trends and develop data-driven marketing strategies. I learned that data is meaningless without insight and there are many ways to effectively employ it.
